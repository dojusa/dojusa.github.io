About Jusabe Guedes

Senior Software Engineer @Tink

I’m a software engineer with 7 years of experience in developing web applications. Through these years I had the opportunity to work on amazing projects that made me passionate about performance issues and big challenges. I consider myself a very adaptive person, so learn a new technology won’t be a problem at all.

After several years working with Java and related technologies such as Java EE, Hibernate, EJB, JSF and JPA, I started falling in love with Elixir. The last couple of years I have been building & scaling distributed systems with Elixir in different sectors like e-commerce and telecom.

I also have great expertise about databases involving Oracle, PostgreSQL and ETL transformations using Pentaho Data Integration.
